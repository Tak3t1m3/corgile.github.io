Vehicle Valuation System

A comprehensive Django-based application for managing vehicle valuations and generating detailed reports. This system allows users to create, manage, and generate PDF reports for both valuation and mechanical assessments of vehicles.

AMSA Seed Platform

A Django-based web application designed to showcase and manage commercial agricultural seed products. This platform serves as both a product catalog and information hub for professional growers and agricultural businesses. A comprehensive agricultural data analytics platform empowering Jamaica's agricultural sector with real-time market analytics, interactive data visualization, and customized insights. Built with Django and featuring interactive charts for commodity prices, market trends, and agricultural forecasting to support data-driven decision making.
